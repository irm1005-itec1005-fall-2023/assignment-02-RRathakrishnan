Portfolio

Project 1

"Selena in Abstract" was made to integrate Selena Gomez's authenticity and creative risks, both of which inspire me. Abstract digital artwork allowed me to experiment and show off my skills with digital tools. The piece shows my determination to reject norms and push limits in unconventional ways.

Project 2

"The 'Urban Mirage' digital artwork, seeks to highlight my prowess in digital editing and understanding of illusion. This piece of art raises questions about viewers' perceptions of reality and inspires awe and fascination. Its inclusion in the portfolio seeks to emphasise my creativity, ingenuity, and technical expertise while also showcasing my ability to produce visually appealing and thought-provoking works that push limits and spark imagination."

Project 3

I wanted to make something for my portfolio that would show my ability as a candidate for the I.T program. Due to its iconic prominence and exquisite design, I chose the Eiffel Tower as my topic. I concentrated on representing the tower's intricate structure in my drawing, paying close attention to detail in order to create an aesthetically pleasing art piece. This attention to detail demonstrates my commitment to the craft of art as well as my ability to capture precision and (somethimg) in my work, which I believe will serve me well in the program.
